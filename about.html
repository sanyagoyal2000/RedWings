Our main objectives at Red Wings are:

  • Safe and Adequate Blood Supplies
  • Reduced Turnaround Time
  • Preventing Wastage of Blood
  • Networking of Blood Banks
  • Donor Repository

It is our mission to make the best use of contemporary technologies in delivering a promising web portal to bring together all the blood donors and blood banks in India; thereby fulfilling every blood request in the country.

We, at Red Wings, are a community of blood donors and blood banks who aspire to provide easy access to the blood banks and related hospitals to both recipients and donors. Donors can join us and look for nearest blood banks to their location. People in need of blood can search for blood banks near them. In order to provide easy access of blood to those in need, we also share your details with the blood bank you are approaching to reduce the hassle of documentation when in need. We also provide a list of blood banks associated with us to ensure the authenticity and their contact information in case of emergencies. Anyone can associate with us and help us fulfil our objectives and save a life. We also regularly organize blood donation camps so that those willing to donate blood can get the opportunity to do their part for the betterment of society.
